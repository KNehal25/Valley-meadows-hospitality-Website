Valley Meadows Hospitality

We have earned our name by developing one-of-a-kind properties and experiences that capture the essence of each place.

We are the owners of luxury properties: Shangri-la Jungle Resort, Valley Meadows Farms, and our restaurants Fat Fish Restaurant & Bar, St. Anthony's Beach Shack and Bobby's Fat Fish. Our vision is to create places of dreams. We are committed to keeping loyal to our Goan culture and including it as much as possible in all we do.

We feel that the notion of luxury is being redefined and that it is becoming increasingly centered on characteristics such as space, nature, silence, time for oneself, and discrete service while still paying close attention to detail.
